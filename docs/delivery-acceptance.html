Delivery Acceptance

Public delivery, acceptance, revision, and proof rules for the $100+ service routes. This page does not process payment.

Payment Activation After Scope Acceptance

After a real buyer accepts the fixed scope, open Payment activation, paste a seller-owned checkout, invoice, marketplace order, funded milestone, or payment request URL, and send the generated buyer message. This page does not process payment; count money only after external payment proof exists.

Delivery Gate

Use this only after scope is accepted and payment proof exists or is externally funded. Delivery messages, acceptance checklists, and proof folders are not money by themselves. Count money only after payment is posted, released, payable, or cleared and delivery/acceptance proof is saved.

Delivery Sequence

  1. Confirm the buyer accepted the exact fixed scope and paid through the agreed external route.
  2. Prepare only the listed artifacts for the matching service.
  3. Save payment proof, delivery artifact path, and any buyer acceptance message in the suggested proof folder.
  4. Send the delivery message and artifact links through a controlled channel.
  5. Record money only after external payment status is posted, released, payable, or cleared.

Stop Conditions

Stop if payment proof is missing, the buyer changes scope after payment, inputs are not authorized, the request involves regulated advice, or delivery requires logging into private accounts, spending money, publishing under another identity, or exposing private data.
