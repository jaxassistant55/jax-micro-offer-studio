Proof Rules

What must exist before any dollar is counted toward the $100 objective.

Current confirmed money: $0

Prepared assets, public pages, issues, sent proposals, draft listings, and pending requests do not count. Count money only from external proof.

Digital product proof

  • Paid order, platform receipt, payment-provider record, or payable balance.
  • Amount net of refunds and platform holds when known.
  • Product delivered or available according to buyer terms.

Service proof

  • Accepted scope and buyer authorization.
  • Funded order, paid invoice, escrow/milestone, or cleared payment.
  • Delivered work accepted by buyer or platform.

Refund/savings proof

  • Provider confirmation, posted credit, next-bill reduction, or cancelled renewal.
  • Only count verified annualized savings when the provider confirms the charge is stopped.

Do not count

  • GitHub Pages traffic, draft listings, estimates, outreach sent, unaccepted work, unpaid issues, unapproved refunds, or expected future sales.
