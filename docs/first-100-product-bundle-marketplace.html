Custom implementation, ongoing support, extra revisions, account login work, paid ads, scraping behind logins, regulated advice, credential handling, or unsupported income/outcome guarantees.

This listing copy is not payment proof. The seller should deliver first-100-product-bundle.zip only after a real buyer accepts the product-transfer terms and pays through a seller-controlled checkout, invoice, marketplace order, funded milestone, or payment request. Count money only after payment is posted, released, payable, or cleared.

Seller Publish Checklist

# Seller Publish Checklist

1. Choose a seller-owned marketplace, checkout, invoice, funded milestone, or payment request route.
2. Confirm tax, payout, refund, and identity settings in the seller account.
3. Upload the cover image from this packet.
4. Paste the title, subtitle, price, description, tags, license note, delivery note, and support boundary from marketplace_listing_fields.csv.
5. Do not upload the private paid ZIP publicly unless the platform gates it behind completed payment.
6. If the platform supports gated files, upload first-100-product-bundle.zip privately from the local non_bounty folder.
7. If the platform does not gate files, collect payment first and deliver the ZIP through a controlled private channel.
8. Save buyer order/payment proof with provider, date, amount, status, and order/reference id.
9. Deliver the ZIP privately and save delivery proof.
10. Count $0 until funds are posted, released, payable, or cleared.
