How To Use

  1. Create or open a payment route you control: checkout link, invoice link, marketplace order, funded milestone, or payment account request.
  2. Paste that URL below, then optionally add buyer name, seller contact, and due date.
  3. Copy the generated message for the matching service and send it only through your controlled seller channel.
  4. Do not start work until the buyer accepts scope and the external payment route is funded, posted, released, payable, or otherwise saveable as proof.
  5. Count $0 until payment proof exists; then count only verified posted/released/payable money net of refunds and holds.

Stop Conditions

Stop if the payment URL is not yours, the buyer has not accepted scope, the work requires private credentials or regulated advice, the buyer asks you to post private data publicly, or payment proof cannot be saved.
