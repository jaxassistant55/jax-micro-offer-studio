Ready To Buy: Website Audit Microservice

This page removes catalog browsing and routes a buyer straight to a scoped inquiry for a $150 service. It still does not process payment; payment must move through a seller-controlled external route after scope is accepted.

Use This When

A buyer wants public website, landing-page, accessibility, mobile, copy, and qa quick-win audits. and can provide public or authorized non-sensitive inputs for scoping.

Buyer Steps

  1. Open the prefilled inquiry and confirm the requested scope, deadline, delivery preference, and acceptance proof.
  2. Use only public, synthetic, or authorized input. Do not post credentials, private payment data, tax identifiers, customer files, or regulated private details.
  3. Wait for seller acceptance of the fixed scope and payment/proof route before expecting paid delivery.
  4. Pay only through an external seller-controlled checkout, invoice, marketplace order, funded milestone, or payment account.
  5. Count the work as money only after the payment is posted, released, payable, or cleared.

Delivery Boundary

Prepared public pages and sample previews are not the full paid delivery. Paid custom work or paid bundle transfer starts only after scope and payment are accepted.

Proof required: Count $0 unless a real buyer accepts scope, pays through a user-controlled route, receives delivery, and payment is posted/released/payable/cleared.
